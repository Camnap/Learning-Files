Mandarin Chinese Restaurants

2206 Eglinton Ave East
Toronto ON

The Review

Mandarin has all of the usual Chinese dishes you would expect, but they also have a very generous selection of other dishes such as a Roast Beef and Pizza, and they do a good job with those as well. Lots of good desserts and many outlets over the Southern Ontario area.

Mandarin Favourites
